# 🎙️ Vocalis — Intelligent AI Interview Simulation Platform

Vocalis is a high-performance product designed to transform how students and professionals prepare for the hiring bar. It’s not just a mock interview tool; it’s an interactive, AI-driven ecosystem that simulates the pressure and depth of elite-tier job interviews.

---

## ✨ Signature Features

### 🌊 Cinematic Interaction Design
The platform features a **"Cyber-Dark" Cinematic UI** that creates a calm yet high-tech preparation environment, allowing users to focus entirely on their performance.

### 🎙️ Natural Voice Dialogue
Experience fluid, zero-latency conversations. Vocalis uses a persistent **WebSocket architecture** to ensure your dialogue with the AI feels human, allowing for natural pauses, follow-up questions, and deep technical probing.

### 👁️ Behavioral Vision Intelligence
Our AI doesn't just listen—it watches. Using advanced vision tracking, Vocalis monitors your physical presence in real-time:
- **Neural Gaze Estimation**: Tracks eye contact and visual confidence throughout the session.
- **Posture & Body Language**: Analyzes your physical stance to ensure a professional demeanor.
- **Real-Time Facial Monitoring**: Detects engagement levels and facial expressions to provide nuanced behavioral coaching.
- **High-Definition Session Recording**: Automatically record your entire session (video & audio). Download the file instantly for self-review and progress tracking.
- **Privacy-First Processing**: All vision analysis and recordings happen locally on your device—your data never leaves your browser.

### 📊 Professional Performance Analytics
Every session ends with a comprehensive **Elite Feedback Report**.
- **Student Dashboard**: Track your growth over time with interactive performance charts.
- **Skill Gap Analysis**: Identify exactly where your technical knowledge or behavioral responses need work.
- **Admin Intelligence**: A full-scale dashboard for administrators to monitor community growth, review sessions, and manage student inquiries through a dedicated **Intelligence Inbox**.
- **Secure Ecosystem**: Seamless and secure access via **Google OAuth 2.0**, ensuring your professional profile and interview data are always protected.

---

## 🎙️ The Vocalis Experience

1.  **Sync Your Intelligence**: Simply upload your PDF resume. The system uses deep parsing to understand your stack and experience instantly.
2.  **Define Your Target**: Tell the AI where you're headed (e.g., "Software Engineer at Google"). The interview track is customized for that specific role.
3.  **Engage in Simulation**: Step into a private, high-fidelity interview session. The AI adjusts its difficulty dynamically—if you're doing well, it goes deeper; if you're struggling, it provides guidance.
4.  **Master the Bar**: Review your detailed analysis and watch your performance trend upward on your dashboard.

---

## 🛡️ Privacy & Professionalism

- **Privacy First**: All video processing and behavioral tracking happen **locally** in your browser. Your recording is yours to keep and is never stored on our servers.
- **Cinematic Aesthetic**: From the floating pill navigation to the film-grain textures, every pixel of Vocalis is designed to feel like a premium, world-class product.
